Raven's Progressive Matrices Versions

Standard Progressive Matrices (SPM)

The original Raven's test, designed for general populations, assessing abstract reasoning through 60 progressively complex pattern-based problems.

Coloured Progressive Matrices (CPM)

Developed for children and individuals with learning difficulties, using simpler patterns and colors across 36 items to measure fluid intelligence.

Advanced Progressive Matrices (APM)

Designed for adults with higher cognitive abilities, featuring 36 complex items to evaluate advanced abstract reasoning and problem-solving.

Standard Progressive Matrices Plus (SPM Plus)

An enhanced version of the SPM with increased difficulty, used for more precise measurement in average to above-average intelligence ranges.

Parallel Progressive Matrices

A parallel form of the SPM, created for retesting scenarios to minimize practice effects while maintaining comparable difficulty and structure.

Raven's Matrices for Gifted Children

Tailored for identifying gifted children, this version uses challenging patterns to assess high-level reasoning in younger populations.

Shortened Progressive Matrices

A condensed version of the SPM, designed for quicker assessments while still measuring abstract reasoning with fewer items.

Raven's Matrices for Clinical Use

Adapted for clinical settings, this version assesses cognitive impairments with simplified patterns and structured administration.

Computerized Progressive Matrices

A digital adaptation of the SPM, offering automated scoring and adaptive difficulty for efficient and accurate cognitive assessment.
